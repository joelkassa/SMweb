Why this matters

A largely invisible crisis, carried mostly in silence.

Ethiopian youth face a mental health system that is stretched thin, and a culture where stigma keeps many from ever asking for help. Specialists are scarce and concentrated in a few cities, private care is unaffordable for most students, and the result is a wide and well-documented treatment gap. We built Safe Minds Ethiopia to close that gap — starting with the young people who are hardest to reach and least likely to ask.
