Platform launch

We're building something that matters.

Our confidential telemedicine platform — connecting young Ethiopians to licensed psychiatrists through secure, anonymous voice and video sessions — is coming soon. In the meantime, you can learn about our programs or reach out directly.

If you are in crisis right now

Safe Minds Ethiopia does not provide emergency or crisis intervention services. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact the nearest hospital emergency department, call 911 (if available), or reach out to a trusted adult, family member, or teacher. You are not alone, and help is available.
